SIGNIFY - VIRTUAL TEACHING PORTAL

  • A full fledged web application for organisation to teach and to assign the assignments to the students. This web application has 3 modules i.e, for student, faculty and the admin.

  • The active classes are displayed in student and teacher module. Active quizes are displayed on student's module.

  • Parallely the performance of each student in the quiz is also tracked down and the list of performance of students is shown to the teacher and individial progress and performace to the student.
